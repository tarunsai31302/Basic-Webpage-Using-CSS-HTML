This site is the collection of different characters and groups from different comic book publishing companies

Marvel

Marvel Comics is the brand name and primary imprint of Marvel Worldwide Inc., formerly Marvel Publishing, Inc. and Marvel Comics Group, a publisher of American comic books and related media. It owns publishes popular characters like Avengers, X-men, Fantastic Four, and many other popular.It is currently owned by Walt Disney company.

DC

DC Comics, Inc. is an American comic book publisher and the flagship unit of DC Entertainment. It publishes popular characters like Batman , Superman , Justice League and other heroes and groups. It also owns characters from other comic book companies like Wildstorm, Milestone. DC Entertainment is a subsidiary of the Warner Bros, which itself is a subsidiary of AT&T.

Image

Image Comics is an American comic book publisher and is the third largest comic book and graphic novel publisher. It publishes Invincible, G.I.Joe, Walking Dead among others. It is mostly popular for it's grittier and darker version of the comics.

Valiant

Valiant Comics is an American publisher of comic books and related media. The company was founded in 1989 by former Marvel Comics editor-in-chief Jim Shooter. Shadowman, Bloodsport are some of the characters from this publication.
